How to Redeem Codes in Grow a Garden

  1. Open Settings: Click the gear icon in the top-left corner of the screen.
  2. Find Code Box: In the settings menu that appears, scroll to the very bottom.
  3. Enter Code: Type or paste your code into the text box under "Redeem Codes".
  4. Claim Rewards: Click the Claim button to receive your items (seeds, eggs, or cosmetics).
